MediClean

MediClean is a surface disinfectant developed for critical and non-critical areas— especially popular in hospitals. Its active ingredient, Sodium Dichloro Salt (NaDCCa), reacts with water to release a potent disinfectant layer that lasts for hours, ensuring continuous protection.
